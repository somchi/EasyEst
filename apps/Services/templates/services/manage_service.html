How Service work on EasyEstate

On EasyEstate you can either Buy or Sell house related services

How To Sell Service(s)

  •     * After you have signup on EasyEstate
  •     * You will have create a service profile by click Create Service Profile
  •     * After creating your profile, specifying the kind of service your provide,
          prospective client will then be able to find you when your service is sort on EasyEstate

That easy right!

Note: Be sure to provide the right information that will give to a competitive advantage.

How to buy Service

  •     * Search for the Service you seek for
  •     * Contact the Provider (seller) either by email or buy phone call
  •     * Communicate with the seller
  •     * Pay after the job has been done

That easy right!

Note: Ensure to meet in a place that is deemed safe if need be, for the safety of both parties.

How to find service providers (sellers)

  •     * You can create a custom request i.e a detail of what you are in need of, and allow sellers contact you based on your request and you decide who to pick, by click on Custom Service Request
  •     * You can also find sellers by clicking on Find a service from Manage Service Page
  •     * You can as well find seller from the service categories on the Home Page
