WHY JOIN OUR COMMUNITY

There are a whole lot of reasons you have to join are community

Develop Africa

The goal is to improve the quality of technical services in Africa and this is achieved by improving the quality of professionals as well as providing assurances on the capacity of these professionals. We provide you with an opportunity to be a part of this revolution

Exposure

Professionals get exposed to those who they need to notice them which are the companies and prospective clients. The companies also get exposed to those who they want to notice them which is basically everybody in the solar energy systems value chain.

Quality of Service

We partner with reputable companies across Africa to offer world class trainings and we get the best consultants from around the world to review the quality of trainings being offered.

Create Jobs

The QSA community makes young people employable through trainings and certifications. It also empowers them to start their own businesses which offer high quality services using a lean start up model. This is because they have access to all the human resources and products they need on the QSA platform.
