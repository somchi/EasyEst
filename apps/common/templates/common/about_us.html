About EasyEstate

EASYESTATE.COM.NG, a product of Judan technologies, is primarily;

An online real estate platform which connects property(lands and housing)owners, agents and brokers to prospective clients willing to buy, lease or rent these properties.

An online platform where house maintenance service providers such as Plumbers, Electricians, Painters, Surveyors, Architects, Real Estate Valuers, Carpenters, Interior decorators etc gets connected to clients who need their services.

Our goal is to effectively solve the problem of property owners and house maintenance service providers - prospective clients hectic connection involved in the business of real estate, and providing a common marketplace for them using the power of technology
